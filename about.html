About Sustainable Charcoal Briquettes Paser Regency

We are dedicated to producing premium, eco-friendly charcoal briquettes in Paser Regency, Indonesia. Our commitment is to quality, sustainability, and empowering local communities.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality, sustainable charcoal briquettes that support clean cooking and grilling, while fostering positive impact for people and the planet.

Our Values

  • Sustainability: We use responsible sourcing and production methods to minimize environmental impact.
  • Quality: Our briquettes are engineered for long-lasting, clean burning and consistent performance.
  • Community: We partner with local communities, creating jobs and supporting economic growth in Paser Regency.

Why Choose Us?

Our charcoal briquettes are trusted by restaurants and households across Indonesia for their efficiency, low ash, and eco-friendly production. Every purchase helps support sustainable practices and local livelihoods.
